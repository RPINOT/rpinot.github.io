Junior Professor in Machine Learning

I am a junior professor in the department of mathematics at Sorbonne University. I hold a chair on the mathematical foundation of computer and data science within the LPSM research unit. I am also an active member of the Responsible AI initiative within the Sorbonne Center for Artificial Intelligence (SCAI).

From 2021 to 2023, I was a postdoctoral researcher at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, where I worked with Pr. Rachid Guerraoui and Pr. Anne-Marie Kermarrec within the Ecocloud Research Center. From 2017 to 2020 I completed my PhD in Computer Science at PSL University (Paris Dauphine) and Université Paris Saclay (CEA LIST) where I was advised by Pr. Jamal Atif, Dr. Florian Yger, and Dr. Cédric Gouy-Pailler.

Feel free to navigate my website to learn more about my Research, Teaching and Outreach activities. You are also welcome to check out the website of the Responsible AI initiative at SCAI!
